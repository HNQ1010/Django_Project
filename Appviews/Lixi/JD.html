JOB DESCRIPTION

☛ Warehouse Manager

  • Controlling processes daily orders in creating picking list, packing and handing over orders to 3PL.
  • Ensure orders are being filled and handed over timely and accurately.
  • Prevents product damage and shrinkage, accidents, and occupational hazards.
  • Effectively monitors flow of goods through the inventory management system and warehouse physical storage.
  • Maintain a clean and safe work environment.
  • Staffing and managing the fulfillment employees to include all permanent and seasonal staff.
  • Provides clear direction and general guidance to the department in the formulation and implementation of procedures,
    and ensures that these are translated and enforced into action to meet operational goals.
  • Develops and implements business process initiatives to optimize departmental operations in accordance with overall business objectives.
  • Prepares necessary SOPâ€™s for fulfillment processes.
  • Help analyze current operations and make recommendations on ways to improve
  • Manages and provides weekly reporting.
  • Assist in growing our customer base by providing excellent service.
  • Maintains the highest standards of customer service by immediately attending to and resolving customer issues related to warehouse operation.

☛ Warehouse Supervisor/ Warehouse Team Leader

  • Achieve high levels of satisfaction through excellence in warehouse processes (receiving, QC, picking, packing, handover, ..).
  • Measure and report the effectiveness of warehousing activities and employees performance
  • Organize and maintain inventory and storage area.
  • Ensure processes and inventory transactions (inbound, outbound) accuracy.
  • Communicate job expectations from higher manager and coach employees.
  • Determine staffing levels and assign workload.
  • Interface with customers or related departments to answer questions or solve problems.
  • Maintain items record, document necessary information and utilize reports to project warehouse status.
  • Identify areas of improvement and establish innovative or adjust existing work procedures and practices.
  • Confer and coordinate activities with other departments when necessary.

☛ Warehouse Staff

  • Loading and unloading delivery vehicles.
  • Accepting delivery of inventory.
  • Counting and confirming inventory.
  • Inspecting inventory for damage and faults.
  • Communicating errors to relevant parties.
  • Marking and labeling stock.
  • Storing inventory in accessible manner.
  • Loading and wrapping stock on pallets.
  • Monitor flow of product into processing machinery
  • Verify compliance with quality, safety, production standards, work rules, and efficient operation of equipment
  • Ensuring and maintain cleanliness, tidiness and safety of work environment.

    • ★ Based on warehouse processes and procedures, employees are assigned other tasks by team leader/ managers.

☛ 3PL Management Leader

  • Direct, optimize and coordinate full order cycle
  • Plan routes and process shipments
  • Keep track of delivery times, transport costs and efficiency to meet cost, productivity, accuracy and timeliness targets.
  • Developing and sustaining long-standing relationships with company-approved vendors.
  • Maintain metrics and analyze data to assess performance and implement improvements of 3PL's services.
  • Establishing standards by which to assess the performance of approved vendors.
  • Conducting research on available vendors to determine which vendors offer the best pricing and service quality.
  • Conducting interviews with new vendors and informing approved vendors of their responsibilities and obligations.
  • Resolve any arising problems or complaints related to 3PL services.
  • Comply with laws, regulations and ISO requirements

☛ 3PL Management staff

  • Direct supervisory responsibility and first point of contact for the 3PL.
  • Develop strong and integrated relationships with our 3PL warehouse partners and communicate our business model accurately to them.
  • Oversee daily 3PL transactions to ensure all required processes are completed in a timely manner; especially outbound shipments.
  • Resolve discrepancies as needed and perform cycle counts when appropriate.
  • Thorough understanding of 3PL warehouse operations and administrative functions. Strong working knowledge of shipping, receiving, inventory management and pricing structure a plus.
  • Preparation of purchasing requisitions/orders to ensure on-time delivery of products.
  • Liaise with suppliers to chase up outstanding orders.
  • Liaising with customer services, QC, returns and warehousing functions.
  • Ensure relevant paperwork flow is consistent and timely.
  • Collating data for KPIs on supplier delivery performance and schedules.
  • Develop and maintain 3PLs relationships to ensure that communication and supply of information is at the highest level both internally and externally.
  • Accurately maintain the appropriate administration of processes for reporting and tracking. Responding to internal and external queries.
  • Updating systems accurately.
