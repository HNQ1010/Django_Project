WAREHOUSE RE-ALLOCATION PROJECT

    ★ Issues

    • WH-Allocation was organized in the simple form including:

      • Line - Bay - Position
      • Line: It's named with letter (A, B, C, ...)
      • Bay: Based on shelves format (6).
      • Position: Order start from 1 and gradually increase to the end. (1 to 24, 28, 32, ..)
    • For example: A01.06
      • Line: A
      • Bay 01: 01
      • Position: 06

      ★ The problems are:

      • New staff can't be familiar with warehouse allocation immediately → make difficulty when serving warehouse operations: put away, picking, packing, ...
      • There's no format for allocation arrangement when expanding warehouse area.

    ★ Next steps

    • Re-arrange warehouse allocation following the format:

      • WH Name - Area - Line - Bay - Level - Position

      with line's format are named:

      • F : Fixed area
      • M : Moving (free) area
    • For example: SG-F0101-11
      • WH Name: Saigon Packing
      • Area: Fixed area
      • Line: 01
      • Bay: 01
      • Level: 1
      • Position: 1

      ★The advantages of the new warehouse allocation format are:

      • Easy for understanding, guiding, introducing or even expanding in larger warehouse.
      • Each letter contains its meaning, it makes significant in warehouse allocation.
      • New staff or viewers can easily contain, understand allocation's format and get the real positions inside warehouse.

      • https://drive.google.com/file/d/1LZ7YBmiWeDnSSYIk5962Fw9fZmI1RtcE/view?usp=sharing
