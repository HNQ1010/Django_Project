WAREHOUSE RE-LOCATION PROJECT

    ★ Issues

    • Address: Taiyo Nissin Building, 100, Nguyễn Thị Tư, Q9, HCM.
    • SG Packing warehouse is placed at inconvenience place:
      • The place is in a sea port area → container truck get in and out regularly → high risks for traffic.
      • The place is affected by flood, sea level.
      • The distance is far away from center → 3PL vendors (express) face issues when coming to the warehouse → we can't utilise vendor's services.
      • Store usages are divided in different floors → goods are not concentrated in a area → make additional movement when processing goods.
      • Building policies is limited in:
        • Number of people that can enter into building → force us use their labor with high cost.(13.63% higher than other Vendors).
        • They don't work on weekend → In case we have to operate our activities, it's a bottle neck.
      • The price is quite high (140k/ m2) compare with some other places.(Tan Binh, Tan Tao, Vinh Loc industrial areas, ... →110-120k/ m2).
      • The warehouse storage is not many available for receiving goods or extending for function areas (picking, packing, handover, ...) in peak days.
