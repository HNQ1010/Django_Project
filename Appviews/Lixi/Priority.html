        • 1. Packing Process
          • Old Process Expected Process

          Reference document:
          https://drive.google.com/file/d/1KdmtorUqYmEIZ0nkzhrMS1v7IMopcC5a/view?usp=sharing

          At this time, when doing packaging process, staff have to look into product's information, identify them and collect them together. This lead to:

          • Easily pick wrong products for each order.
          • Difficulty when PICs are absented, new/ other employees spend long time joining in packaging process.

          The expected process:

            Create input place for confirming product's barcode when packaging orders. This lead to

            • When packer pack order, they have to scan to confirm it's right product (with its barcode) → minimize probability in picking wrong products of packaging orders.
            • New/ other employees have no confusion when joining packaging process. They don't have to ask others for information or get confuse when packaging orders.
            • When process is updated, the actions are similar → each employee can obtain the same productivity and we aren't depend on personal experience in packing process.
        • 2. Handover
          • Old Process Expected Process

          Reference document:
          https://drive.google.com/file/d/1NU56I7vnPQDVUv-Ks6XLk3MozoGdqCsz/view?usp=sharing

          At this time, when handing over orders to 3PL, we have to scan one by one order to confirm those orders was
          handed over to 3PL. This lead to:

          • Missing handover list when confirm to 3PL.
          • Manual process handing over to 3PL (confirm to change status one by one order).
          • Difficulty in controlling handover process to 3PL.

          The expected process:

            Create Handover list before handing over to 3PL. This lead to:

            • Identify number of orders will hand over to each 3PL.
            • Confirm a list instead of one by one order.
            • Control hand over situation before handing over to 3PL.
        • 3. Re-check B2B and B2C Picking list
          • Old Process Expected Process

          Reference document:
          https://drive.google.com/file/d/1skEPA9o13pRhdp3aXMIBan-ZMwOthewZ/view?usp=sharing

          At this time, when doing re-check process for B2B and B2C orders, staff have to look into each product's information,
          identify that product and confirm picked items for that products. This lead to:

          • Spend a long time for re-check process → if it's B2B order with large number of items, we can spend whole day or a few days to re-check it.
          • By looking into product's label for identifying it → easily pick wrong products to confirm.

          The expected process:

          Create input place for searching re-check products → after scanning product's barcode → direct to re-check product. This lead to:

          • Reduce re-check time and manual processes by multiple times.
          • Increase accuracy in picking right product to re-check.
        • 4. B2B Picking List
